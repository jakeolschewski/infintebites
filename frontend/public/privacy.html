1. Introduction

Thank you for using InfantBites (“InfantBites,” “we,” “our,” or “us”). We create tools to help parents and caregivers make confident, informed decisions about their baby’s feeding and development.

We know that anything related to your child is deeply personal. This Privacy Policy explains in clear, plain language:

  • What information we collect
  • How and why we use it
  • When we share it
  • What rights and choices you have
Short version: We collect only what we need to run the service, we don’t sell your personal information, and you can ask us to delete or export your data.

2. Who We Are

InfantBites is a digital product that provides baby feeding and development guidance, personalized plans, and curated product recommendations, including affiliate product bundles.

3. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to information we collect when you:

  • Visit or use our website, web app, and associated tools;
  • Interact with our emails, forms, or support channels;
  • Click on or interact with product and affiliate recommendations within InfantBites.

This policy does not cover third-party websites or services that link to, or from, InfantBites. Those services have their own privacy policies.

4. Information We Collect

4.1 Information You Provide to Us

We collect information that you choose to give us, for example when you generate a plan or contact us.

Baby’s age and concern
When you use InfantBites, you can enter your baby’s age in months and select a concern (such as feeding, solids, growth, or other topics). We design this input to be non-identifying on its own.
Contact information (optional)
If you choose to receive your plan by email, or contact us for support, we collect your email address and any information you include in your message.
Data request information
When you submit a privacy request (such as export or deletion), we collect the email address and request details you provide so we can identify your records and respond.

4.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you use the Service, some information is automatically collected, for example:

  • IP address and approximate location (city or region, not precise address);
  • Browser type, language, device type, and operating system;
  • Referring URL (how you arrived at our website);
  • Pages and features you use, time and date of visits, and interactions (clicks, scrolls).

4.3 Affiliate Interaction Data

When we show you product bundles or affiliate recommendations, we may track:

  • Which product links you click;
  • When those clicks happen;
  • Whether a partner reports a purchase attributed to our referral (where applicable).

We use this primarily to understand which recommendations are helpful and to receive credit from partners.

5. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and personalize the Service.
    Generate tailored guidance and plans based on your inputs, and show relevant product bundles.
  • To communicate with you.
    Send your plan by email if requested, respond to questions, and provide important updates about the Service or this policy.
  • To understand what’s working.
    Analyze aggregated and de-identified usage data to improve content, design, and product recommendations.
  • To support affiliate relationships.
    Track clicks on affiliate links and measure performance so we can maintain partnerships and keep InfantBites sustainable.
  • To maintain safety and integrity.
    Prevent, detect, and investigate fraud, abuse, security incidents, or violations of our terms.
  • To comply with legal obligations.
    Respond to lawful requests from authorities, keep required records, and comply with applicable laws.

6. Legal Bases (Where Applicable)

In some regions (for example, the European Economic Area or United Kingdom), we process personal information only when we have a valid legal basis, such as:

  • Consent – for example, when you ask us to email your plan.
  • Contract – where processing is necessary to provide the Service you request.
  • Legitimate interests – such as improving the Service, preventing fraud, and running analytics, provided those interests are not outweighed by your rights and interests.
  • Legal obligation – where we must process or retain data to comply with the law.

7. When We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share information in these limited situations:

  • Service providers. With trusted vendors who help us host, maintain, analyze, or support the Service (for example: cloud hosting, analytics, email delivery). They may access your information only to perform work on our behalf.
  • Affiliate partners. We may share aggregated or de-identified statistics about clicks and conversions. We do not share your email address with affiliates unless you explicitly agree.
  • Legal and safety. If required by law, or if we believe it is reasonably necessary to protect the rights, safety, or property of InfantBites, our users, or others.
  • Business transfers. If we undergo a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of our assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction, subject to appropriate safeguards.

8. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We may use cookies and similar technologies (such as local storage or pixels) to:

  • Remember your preferences and basic session details;
  • Measure how many people use certain features;
  • Track affiliate link clicks and performance;
  • Improve the speed, reliability, and experience of the Service.

You can typically control cookies in your browser settings. If you block or delete cookies, some parts of the Service may not function properly.

9. Data Retention

We keep your information only for as long as needed to:

  • Provide and improve the Service;
  • Support affiliate operations and accurate reporting;
  • Comply with legal, tax, or accounting obligations;
  • Resolve disputes and enforce our agreements.

When information is no longer needed, we either delete it or anonymize it so it can no longer be associated with you.

10. Children’s Privacy

InfantBites is designed for use by adults (parents and caregivers), not children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.

The only child-related detail we ask for is age in months, which we treat as non-identifying. Please do not share your child’s full name, address, or other identifying information with us.

If you believe that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us using the details below so we can delete it.

11. Your Rights & Choices

Depending on where you live, you may have some or all of the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access – request a copy of the information we hold about you.
  • Correction – request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion – ask us to delete your information, subject to legal exceptions.
  • Portability – request your information in a structured, commonly used format.
  • Restriction – ask us to limit the way we use your information in certain cases.
  • Objection – object to certain types of processing, such as direct marketing or some analytics, where permitted by law.
  • Withdraw consent – where we rely on consent, you may withdraw it at any time.

You can exercise these rights by contacting us or using the form below. We may need to verify your identity before fulfilling your request.

Note: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights.

12. Submit a Data Request

Use this form to request an export of your data or to ask us to delete the personal information we hold under your email address. We’ll respond as soon as reasonably possible and within the time frames required by law.

We’ll use this only to locate your records and reply to your request.

13. How We Protect Your Information

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information from loss, misuse, unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

However, no method of transmitting or storing data is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we do work continuously to improve our safeguards.

14. International Users

If you access InfantBites from outside the country where our servers are located, your information may be transferred across borders and processed in other countries.

Data protection laws in those countries may be different from those in your country of residence. Where required, we take steps to ensure appropriate protections for such transfers.

15. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technologies, or legal requirements.

When we do, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, provide additional notice (for example, a banner on the site or an email).

Your continued use of InfantBites after a change means you accept the updated policy.
